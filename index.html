My Design Process

My approach to design is systematic and user-focused. Here's a glimpse into how I work:

Research

Conducting user research to understand pain points and goals.

Wireframing

Creating layouts to visualize the structure of designs.

Prototyping

Developing interactive prototypes to simulate user journeys.

Testing

Performing usability tests to gather actionable feedback.

Iterating

Refining designs based on test results and stakeholder input.
